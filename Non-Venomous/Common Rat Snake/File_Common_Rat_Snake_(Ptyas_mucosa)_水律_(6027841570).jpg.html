DescriptionCommon Rat Snake (Ptyas mucosa) 水律 (6027841570).jpg

Found rummaging about in our pile of strange garden fibreglass fruit, taken near Ho Chung, Sai Kung.

Eventually it emerged with a very large Asian common toad in it's mouth, hissing and flicking its tail in a very agitated way, so we backed away to let it enjoy its meal.
